Paper EOB Conversion

Our Paper EOB Conversion service transforms physical Explanation of Benefits documents into digital formats that integrate seamlessly with your existing systems. We utilize advanced OCR technology and AI-powered data extraction to ensure accuracy and completeness.

Benefits include:

  • Reduced manual data entry errors
  • Faster payment posting
  • Improved searchability and access to payment information
  • Reduced physical storage requirements
  • Enhanced data security and compliance
